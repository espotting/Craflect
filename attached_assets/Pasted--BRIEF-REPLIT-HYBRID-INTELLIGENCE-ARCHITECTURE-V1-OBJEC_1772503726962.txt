2️⃣ CONFIDENCE SCORE (APPLIQUÉ AUX DEUX LAYERS)

Formule identique pour Global et Workspace.

Volume Score (40%)
volume_score = min(total_videos / 500, 1)
Consistency Score (40%)
consistency_score =
average(
dominant_hook_percentage,
dominant_structure_percentage,
dominant_format_percentage
) / 100
Stability Score (20%)

Simplification V1 acceptable.

Final
confidence =
(0.4 × volume_score) +
(0.4 × consistency_score) +
(0.2 × stability_score)

Affichage en %.

3️⃣ SIGNAL STRENGTH
