🧮 SCORING SYSTEM
Pattern Stability Score

Use entropy-based formula:

stability = 1 - normalized_entropy(hook_distribution)

Higher = more concentrated patterns.

Confidence Score

confidence = min(1, log(total_videos)/log(1000)) × stability_score
