🧮 7️⃣ SCORING SYSTEM
Pattern Stability Score

Mesure concentration des hooks :

stability = 1 - normalized_entropy(hook_distribution)

Plus distribution concentrée → score élevé.

Confidence Score

confidence = min(1, log(total_videos)/log(1000)) * pattern_stability_score
