BILLING PAGE

8. Billing & AI Credits

URL

/billing

Title

Billing & AI Credits

Content

Overview

Craflect operates using a subscription model combined with AI usage credits.

Each subscription plan provides access to the platform along with a monthly allocation of AI credits used to power advanced features.

Subscription Plans

Craflect offers three main subscription tiers designed for creators and teams.

Starter

Designed for individual creators exploring the platform.

Includes platform access and a monthly allocation of AI credits for basic usage.

Pro

Designed for active creators who frequently generate scripts and analyze content trends.

Includes higher AI credit limits and extended platform capabilities.

Scale

Designed for professional creators, teams, or agencies with higher usage requirements.

Includes larger credit allocations and advanced usage capacity.

Full plan comparisons are available on the Pricing section of the website.

AI Credits

AI credits power the core AI features of Craflect.

Credits may be used for actions such as:

• generating scripts
• analyzing content trends
• generating creative opportunities
• creating video blueprints or concepts

Credits are deducted from the user's available balance whenever AI features are used.

Credit Reset

AI credits reset at the beginning of each billing cycle.

Unused credits may expire at the end of the billing cycle depending on the subscription plan.

Usage Transparency

Users can monitor their credit usage from their dashboard.

The dashboard shows:

• credits remaining
• credits used during the billing period
• next credit reset date

Billing Cycle

Subscriptions are billed on a recurring basis depending on the plan selected.

Billing may occur:

• monthly
• annually

Invoices are generated automatically after successful payment.

Payments

Craflect accepts standard online payment methods supported by its payment provider.

Payment information is processed securely.

Plan Changes

Users may upgrade or downgrade their plan at any time.

Changes may take effect immediately or at the next billing cycle.

Cancellation

Users may cancel their subscription at any time from their account settings.

Access to paid features remains active until the end of the billing period.

Support

For any billing questions, contact:

[support@craflect.com](mailto:support@craflect.com)
